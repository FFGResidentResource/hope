SERVICE COORDINATION SIGNUP

As a resident of a Wallick-managed community, you have the privilege of having a Service Coordinator for your housing community! Your Service Coordinator is responsible for helping you, and your family, get connected to available community resources that can help you stabilize your housing and become more self-sufficient. Examples of the services that the Service Coordinator can connect you to include:

  • Eviction prevention assistance
  • Food pantries/giveaways
  • Onsite children's programs
  • Preschool or childcare for young children
  • Assistance with applying for/renewing benefits
  • Employment and training opportunities

However, to take advantage of the services the Service Coordinator provides, we need to get your permission, first, by having you check the appropriate boxes, and sign, below.

I. Right to Privacy

Your Service Coordinator is required to protect your right to privacy. That means that if you work with him/her, s/he is not allowed to share your information with anyone, including your property manager, unless you provide written consent. At the same time, federal and/or state law may require the Service Coordinator to disclose the following:

  • Abuse or neglect of any kind, including physical, mental, financial
  • Endangerment – residents who are a danger to themselves or others
  • Fraudulent activity and other violations of the law
  • Lease violations
  • Information pursuant to a proper court order
