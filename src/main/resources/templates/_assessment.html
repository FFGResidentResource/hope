Instructions:

This assessment form is user to gauge resident's levels of self-sufficiency in six life domains, inlcuding(1) Housing, (2) Money Mangement, (3) Employment, (4) Education, () Network Support, and (6) Household Management. All six domains have a direct impact on resident's ability to remain stable in their housing, which is one of the primary goals of Resident Resources Network.

Assessments of all six domains are to be completed for every resident over the age of 18 with whom a Service Coordinator comes in contact.Assessments should be completed at initial contact with the resident and every six (6) months thereafter.

As you begin the assessment, be sure to:

  • Highlight RRN's/your agency's commitment to self-sufficiency
  • Stress their commitment to cultivating a holistic understanding of their resident's lives to ensure that the most needed serves are provided
  • Explain that the assessment helps to identify other possible ways in which residents may benefit from services, thereby informing additional referrals
  • State upfront that the assessment will be completed at regular intervals in order to ensure that any new needs do not go unaddressed.
  • Mention that residents may decline to answer any question without impacting their access to services.
